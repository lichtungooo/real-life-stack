Real Life Stack is a modular, backend-agnostic toolkit for community apps.

Build apps with pluggable UI modules that work against any data backend:

* Feed — posts, comments, reactions
* Kanban — drag-and-drop task boards
* Calendar — events and scheduling
* Map — location-based community resources
* Contacts — member directory

Switch between data backends without changing your UI:
* Local (IndexedDB, works offline)
* GraphQL (server-based)
* Web of Trust (decentralized, encrypted)

Real Life Stack is free and open-source software (MIT license).
